In King Hall Run, the objective is to survive as long as possible without going down to an F grade.
To move, use the arrow keys accordingly. Left and Right move you left and right, and Up will make you jump.
There are 3 types of obstacles: starships, buses, and scooters. Starships and scooters can be jumped over, but buses must be avoided by moving to the side. Hitting any of these will drop your grade. However, if you see a rubber duck, go hit it, as it boosts your grade!
Now you're ready to play!
